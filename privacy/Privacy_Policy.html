Privacy Policy for Torma2ls - Nupal

Effective Date: July 31, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Torma2ls - Nupal, an application developed by Alex Torma. We are dedicated to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring a secure user experience. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when using our app.

2. Data Collection

  • Types of Data Collected:

    • Website Data: Our app collects text data and images from Drupal, which are stored locally on your device.

  • Method of Collection:

    • Data is collected through detection and scraping as you interact with Drupal via the app.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

The information collected is used to:

  • Improve automation
  • Enhance quality-of-life features
  • Address UI/UX concerns for a better user experience.

4. Data Retention

  • Retention Period:
    • All data is cleared from your device upon reinstallation of the app.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • Third Parties:

    • We do not share any collected data with third parties. All data processing occurs locally on your device.

  • Legal Requirements:

    • We do not share data with anybody at any point in time unless compelled to by law.

6. User Access and Control

  • Access and Control:

    • Since our app does not collect personally identifiable information (PII), there is no need for access, correction, or deletion requests. Users can clear data by reinstalling or uninstalling the app.

  • Opt-Out:

    • If you do not wish to have data collected, you should refrain from using the app. The app does not involve marketing communications.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

  • Usage:
    • Our app does not utilize cookies or other tracking technologies.

8. Data Transfer

  • Transfers:
    • There are no data transfers to external locations; all data processing is performed locally on your device.

9. Children's Privacy

  • Policy:
    • The app is intended solely for use by working professionals. It does not collect personal data from children.

10. Policy Updates

  • Changes:
    • We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.

11. Compliance

  • Regulations:
    • This Privacy Policy is intended to comply with applicable data protection laws both in the United States and internationally.
